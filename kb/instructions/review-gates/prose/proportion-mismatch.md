## Failure mode

The load-bearing idea gets less development than a secondary section because the easier or earlier-written material expanded first.

## Test

Identify the note's core claim, then compare how much support each section contributes to that claim. If the most important part is thinner than secondary material, the proportions are off.

Consider whether the overdeveloped section should be split into its own note.
