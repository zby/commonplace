## Failure mode

The note was generalized from a narrower source, but domain-specific framing leaked through and now overcommits the note to that source domain.

## Test

Use the title and opening paragraph to establish the note's claimed generality. Then scan the body for vocabulary, examples, or metaphors that belong to a narrower domain but are not framed as examples.

Either generalize the residue or mark it explicitly as an illustrative case.
