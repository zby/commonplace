## Failure mode

A specific figure, data point, named study, or empirical claim appears without enough source or context for the reader to evaluate it.

## Test

Scan for numbers, percentages, named studies, or concrete empirical claims. For each one, check whether the note supplies a source and enough local context to make the claim legible.

Unsupported specificity is worse than omitting the number entirely.
