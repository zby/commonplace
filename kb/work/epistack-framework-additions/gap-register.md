# A first-class gap register

Layer: structure.

The competition explicitly wants "where the gaps are." Make *what is not known* a typed artifact (an open question with its blockers), not an implicit absence — otherwise gaps are invisible to retrieval.

A gap is distinct from a [crux](./crux-type.md): a gap is *what is not known* (a neutral map-layer artifact); a crux is *what would move the bottom line* (verdict-relative, attached to an assessment). Both belong, in different layers.
