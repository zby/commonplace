# Derived dashboards and a source-impact command

Source: imported from the [ChatGPT second opinion](./chatgpt-second-opinion.md).

Two navigation surfaces, built once a case has enough claims to need them:

- **Per-inquiry dashboards** — current bottom line, confidence, top cruxes, claims by status, evidence by direction and by independence cluster, missing perspectives, recently changed sources. Consistent with the compiled-index philosophy: **derived, never the source of truth**. The durable sources of truth remain the inquiry, claims, source extractions, cruxes, and assessments.
- **`commonplace-source-impact <source>`** — given a changed/retracted/corrected source, lists the affected extractions, claims, cruxes, assessments, and dashboards, and opens a review queue. This is the command-level expression of [traversable provenance chains](./traversable-provenance-chains.md).
